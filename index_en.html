About Us

Company Background

Antico specializes in creating stylish furniture and complete interior solutions according to clients' wishes and needs. Our high-quality hand-crafted timeless designs are brought to life from initial ideas through sketches and drawings to the final product. The artistic approach and hand-crafting is something that gives an exclusive signature to a unique item that will never appear anywhere else in that form. With meticulous attention to detail, originality and refined sense of style Antiko creates both classic and modern pieces of furniture. Antiko makes beds, wardrobes, chests of drawers, tables, doors, mirror frames, pictures, photos and icons, advertisements, jewelry boxes, church furniture, and relics, as well as anything else you can imagine and cannot buy anywhere else. If you want the wooden house that you have always dreamt of, we can build it for you. If you have any other ideas, simply send a message with a query, we will be more than happy to assist you.
